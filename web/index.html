Observation System

Five Seamless Scales

  • 01 Galactic Field 400 star systems from a single seed — navigable as a live 3D point cloud. Choose by seed or select one of 16 named destinations. 10²² m
  • 02 Stellar System Star + planets on live orbits, driven by a single simulated clock. Moons, rings, day-length — all derived from the seed. 10¹³ m
  • 03 Orbital Approach Eroded planet sphere: oceans, river systems, biomes, craters, volcanoes, cloud layers, atmosphere, aurorae on ~40% of planets. 10⁷ m
  • 04 Atmospheric Layer Terrain streams as you descend. Deterministic weather — storms, rain, lightning; the same storm hits the same coordinates at the same hour. 10³ m
  • 05 Surface Datum Walk, glide, harvest, build. Turtles on beaches, rivers to follow, forests to plant. The orbital view and the footstep are the same world. 10⁰ m
What you will find

A Living World

◎ · terrain
Deterministic Ground

Hydraulic erosion carves rivers and lakes that match the orbital view. Waterfalls at steep drops, biomes from temperature × humidity, L-system trees calibrated per species. Same seed · same planet · always.

⟳ · time
Day · Night · Weather

A single clock drives sunsets, orbital mechanics, moon phases and storm cycles. Aurorae on 40% of planets at night. Lightning follows a deterministic Poisson process — you can predict it from the seed.

◈ · craft
Survival & Craft

Harvest wood, stone, iron, gems. Smelt ore into ingots. Craft structural pieces — planks, walls, roofs, lanterns — or stack Minecraft-style 1 m³ blocks. Grid-snapped, face-stackable. Plant seeds, grow trees.

◐ · move
Locomotion & Comfort

Walk, jump, Iron Man free-flight, paraglider. Comfort vignette anti-nausea, snap or smooth turning. Wrist computer in VR — look at your left hand for inventory, crafting, time controls.

⊙ · combat
Combat & Co-op (opt-in)

Equip a weapon and drone waves begin. Holster it — contemplative explorer restored. Host or join a friend by IP. Same seed, same planet, shared encounter — loot, PvE, bHaptics vest feedback.

◬ · audio
Procedural Audio

All ambiences, footsteps, fauna calls and weather synthesised in real-time from scratch — no audio files. The soundscape is generated from the same seed as the terrain, fauna and weather.
